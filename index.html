Robotic Emotion Modeling

Bio inspired emotions from microbial fuel cells.

Built a working microbial fuel cell stack and mapped voltage dynamics to a circumplex emotion model, letting embedded robots express state dependent behaviors.

Quadruped Locomotion Prototype

PPO training for stable rhythmic gait.

Authored URDF and PyBullet sim, then tuned PPO rewards and curriculum for gait stability and convergence. Achieved consistent average returns across runs with robust stance transitions.

Focus: reward shaping, action scaling, reset strategies, sim to sim reproducibility.

Tools: URDF, PyBullet, PPO, Python, C++.
